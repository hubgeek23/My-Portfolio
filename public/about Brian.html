About Me

My name is Brian. I was born in 1971, Scotland, United Kingdom.

In the beginning

Initally time served Stonemason with 20 years experience working on various sites within the UK Construction industry. Contracts varied with Restoration, new builds and the hewing of ornate sandstone for each project when i was employed as an apprentice and time served Stonemason.

Currently

Since 2009 i am a further education college Lecturer with 14 years experience teaching students from various areas of the globe. Primarily, my role was an instructor teaching employed apprentices the knowledge and practical Stonemasonry skills required for both Banker and building disciplines of the craft.

Throughout my time as Lecturer, i have gained Autodesk certification using AutoCAD, Revit and Inventor Professional ranging from user level to Professional level. The Skills are used to prepare our construction students for careers and university degree courses expanding from Architectural Technology, Project Managers, Civil Engineers and Building Surveyors.

Family life

I am happily married to my lovely wife for 27 years. We have a son, daughter, and of course a wee dog! In my spare time i love to spend time with my family and learn new skills.
